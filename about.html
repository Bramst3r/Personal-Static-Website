About

Hey there! My name is Bram (b. 1998) and I'm a Dutch music producer with a rich background in Electronic Dance Music. I've made songs for different artists and media productions. My notable work comes from a partnership with BMG Production Music. Through this collaboration, my music can be heard on TV and streaming services all over the world.
I also work together with different artists across Europe. On writing camps I get the chance to combine my experience with others to create something completely unique. It's important to keep looking for ways to improve and learn from other people. That's why I also love to experiment with audio production for games or sample packs.
TLDR: I love to create electronic music for all sorts of purposes!

My history in music starts around the age of 10, where I attended drum classes for 4 years. Drums where fun, but I quickly realised that my love for EDM was bigger. That's when I made my first productions inspired by the sounds of Avicii, Hardwell, Armin van buuren, Deadmau5 and the likes.
Not long after that, I got accepted for a 3-year-study in Music Production at the ROC of Amsterdam. That place taught me a lot about the basics and it made me develop my own workflow and style. With that new found knowledge, I wanted to focus more on the technical side of audio and music theory. That's why a bachelor in MediaMusic at ArtEZ Enschede was my next adventure.
After these 2 studies, I'm exploring different opporunities and expanding my network. Whether if it's an album for a band or music for a video game, I love to implement my style in all sorts of projects!
